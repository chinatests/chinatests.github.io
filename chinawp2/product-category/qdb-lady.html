Quai des Bergues

Lady 7-day Manual Winding

The Quai des Bergues Ladies collection reflects elegance and the distinctive style of Czapek watches. All the models in this line feature the 7-day
Caliber SXH1 proprietary mechanical hand-wound movement and the characteristic Czapek fleur-delys hands, which come in Rose Gold, «BlackGold» or «BlueGold» finish. The subdials at 4:30 and 7:30 reveal some technical wizardry: the hands are replaced by two rotating mother-of-pearl discs, an innovative aesthetic approach with a distinctively feminine face. The casing is absolutely stunning and enhances the mechanical beauty of the manual winding movement.
